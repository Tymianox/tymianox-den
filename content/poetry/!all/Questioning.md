Who am I?
What am I?

I am a
Boy.

No.
Girl.

No.
Girlish boy.

No.
Boyish girl.

No.
Between?

No.
Outside?

No.
Both?

No.
None?

No.
Am I human?

...Yes?

What do I call myself?
Do I exist if I don't know what I am?
