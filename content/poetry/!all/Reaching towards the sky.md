I lay in grass,
Counting holes in the sky.
Birds, trees, wind
Drowning -
Below music's waves.
I reach my hand towards the sky
Fingers spread,
I want to grasp it,
Pull down a cloud -
And hold it
And hug it
And love it.


With all my might...
