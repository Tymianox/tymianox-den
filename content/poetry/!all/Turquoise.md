In ocean depths 
In mountain streams
Deep in Earth's crust
I find you - my love

You're a soothing balm
Giving me peace in hardest times
You speak for nature
Your words embrace me when I cry

Oh, how I cherish you
Oh, turquoise - my love
You alone will be with me 
Even in death
