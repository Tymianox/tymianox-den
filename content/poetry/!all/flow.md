Life flows like a river
Sometimes gently
Like a princess
Sometimes vicious
Like a snake
Sometimes there
Is a waterfall

Until we reach
The open sea
And embrace
Death itself
Shake her bony hand
Meet her empty gaze
Kiss her dry lips
